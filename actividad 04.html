2022

The FEP Comeback. The sound balm for the souls of the more than 100,000 people who vibrated again with Un Mundo Distinto and an edition that marked us forever: we celebrated the life of Taylor Hawkins, we danced until dawn with Fatboy Slim, Jungle and Caribou, we discover the best show of 2022 with C.Tangana and we lived the return of The Strokes.

MEMORIES OF THE PAST

2014

The definitive year of Un Mundo Distinto. A historic lineup that was tattooed in the collective memory: Red Hot Chili Peppers, Nine Inch Nails, Phoenix, Los Fabulosos Cadillacs, among others. There were more than 50 thousand people who experienced this edition that signed the big destiny, of Festival Estéreo Picnic.

2016

The year a dictator stole our music and it was the FEP who gave it back with three powerful days starring by Florence + The Machine (the first female headliner in the Festival's history), Mumford & Sons, Snoop Dog, Noel Gallagher , Die Antooord, Kygo and more.

2017

The year The Strokes first played in Colombia was also the year The Weeknd showed us why he was the future of pop. The FEP Casette was the concept of an edition that covered the life of Bowie and the live acts of Justice, Deadmau5, The xx, Flume, Wiz Khalifa, Rancid and the great Totó La Momposina playing in the rain.

2019

The tenth edition of Un Mundo Distinto. A historic anniversary in a new home: Campo de Golf Briceño 18 in Sopó. Kendrick Lamar, Arctic Monkeys, Twenty One Pilots, Sam Smith, Tiësto and the legendary concert by Grupo Niche were some of the highlights of the year that definitively catapulted FEP as one of the best festivals on the continent.
